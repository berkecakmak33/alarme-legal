This Privacy Policy explains how Alarme collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app.

By using Alarme, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect and Use

Alarme is designed to help users wake up using alarms, alarm sounds, wake-up tasks, scan-based challenges, math tasks, memory tasks, and other alarm-dismissal methods.

To provide and improve the Service, the app may store or process the following types of information:

- Alarm times and labels
- Selected alarm sounds
- Selected wake-up tasks
- Repeat days
- App settings
- Theme and language preferences
- Onboarding answers
- Selected scan objects
- Premium subscription status
- Basic app usage and analytics events
- Paywall views and onboarding progress
- Device and app diagnostic information if an error occurs

Most app settings and alarm data are stored locally on your device.

## Camera and Scan Data

Alarme may request camera access for scan-based alarm tasks.

Camera images used for scan verification are processed on your device. Alarme does not upload these camera images to our servers.

Scan recognition may be affected by lighting, camera angle, object visibility, and device conditions.

## Premium Purchases and Subscriptions

Alarme offers free features and optional paid Premium features.

Premium purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple’s App Store and may be managed through your Apple ID account.

We may use third-party purchase management services, such as RevenueCat, to verify subscription status and manage Premium access. These services may process purchase-related identifiers and subscription status information.

We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.
