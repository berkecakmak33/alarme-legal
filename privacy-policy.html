Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Alarme: Alarm & Wake ("Alarme", "the app", "we", or "our") is owned and operated by Hasan Berke Cakmak. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information The App Uses

Alarme may store alarm times, labels, selected sounds, repeat days, wake-up tasks, scan object preferences, onboarding choices, theme and language preferences, and premium status. Most alarm and preference data is stored locally on your device.

Camera And Scan Data

Alarme may request camera access for scan-based alarm tasks. Camera images used for scan verification are processed on your device and are not uploaded to our servers by Alarme.

Notifications And Alarm Permissions

Alarme uses notification and alarm-related permissions to alert you when alarms are due. If these permissions are disabled, alarm features may not work properly.

Purchases And Subscriptions

Alarme offers free features and optional monthly or yearly Premium subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. We may use RevenueCat to verify purchase status and manage Premium access. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Analytics And Diagnostics

The app may use limited analytics or diagnostic information to understand app usage, onboarding progress, paywall views, and errors. This helps us improve stability and user experience. Diagnostics may include device model, iOS version, app version, time of error, and general app configuration.

Third-Party Services

Alarme may use Apple App Store services, RevenueCat, and analytics or diagnostic services. These providers process information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Alarme is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can manage notification, camera, motion, and other app permissions in iOS Settings. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hasanberkecakmak@gmail.com.
